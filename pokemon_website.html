Coalossal

Rock | Fire

The Rock/Fire Pokémon

Evolved from Carkol starting at level 34. It is the final form of Rolycoly.

Steam Engine: When this Pokémon is hit by a Fire-type or Water-type move, its Speed is increased by 6 stages Flame Body: The opponent has a 30% chance of being induced with Burn when using an attack, that requires physical contact, against this Pokémon. Hidden Ability (Available): Flash Fire: Activates when user is hit by a damaging Fire-type move (including Fire-type Hidden Power). Once activated, user’s Fire-type moves deal 1.5 times damage. While this ability is in effect, this Pokémon is immune to damage from Fire-type attacks and Fire-type Hidden Power (accuracy and effect from these moves are ignored). For Fire-type Pokémon with this ability, Will-O-Wisp activates this ability without having an effect. If a non-Fire-type Pokémon has this ability, Will-O-Wisp will activate the ability and will have an effect.
